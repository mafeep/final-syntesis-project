Electric energy

Is the power an atom's changed partides have to cause an action to move and object. The movement of electrons from one atom's to other is what results in electrical energy. As an example, this is a direct electric circuit that consists of a lightbulb and a battery.

Watt

The standard unit of power in the international system of units (SI), equal to the power in a circuit in with current of on ampere flows across a potencial difference of one volt. This is the equation of Watt in function of Ampers and Volts.

Scatter plot

A Scatter plot uses dots to represents values for two different numeric variables. The position of each dot on the horizontal and vertical axis indicates values for an individual data point. This chart materializes the relationship between the number of some species of animal and the passing of the years.

Linear regression

Is a model of the relationship between two things for predictive analysis. In which one is dependent and the other is independent. This is the linear regression of the former scatter plot, it is the line that represents all those points the best way possible.

Renewable energy
