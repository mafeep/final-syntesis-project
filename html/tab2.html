About the observed behavior
  • It can be observed that this linear regression tends to behave as a proportionally direct relationship, it responds to the huge increase of the consumption in the third and the fourth month. That's why it is shown as if, with the pass of the months, it keeps on increasing. As well as you can say the pendant or inclination of the line is medium big, as it is 12. In this case, the months are the independant variable and the kWh the dependant.
  • In general terms, the dots are not so disperse from the linear regression. But they are specially accurate in the first two months, in the other ones you can notice that the true value dots are further away from the grey points. So that, the predictions up to the future could be less or more precise.
  • The estimation, prompted by the equation, enunciates that for a next month the consumed KWH will be: $$y = 5.5 \cdot 6 + 167.3$$ $$y = 167.3$$
  • Given its high tendencies in the last few months, it can be deduced that the usage of electric comodities and lightbulbs has increased significantly. Partly, it was due to visits at home, but still the consumption is high and can and will be reduced. will be through actions such as: showering with cold water, unplugging chargers and other cable when they're not being used, using low power lamps and lightbulbs and turning them off when unused.
